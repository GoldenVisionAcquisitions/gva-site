Whether you own commercial land, represent land sellers, source land deals, or develop car washes — GVA has a clear, structured path for you.

For Land Sellers

Connect Your Commercial Land to a Car Wash Developer

Own commercial land that could be the next car wash location? GVA screens your site against active developer buy boxes and connects you directly to an interested car wash developer — no listing required, no fees to you.

“No listing required. No fees to sellers. GVA screens your land, makes the introduction, and handles everything through to the offer.”
  • 1
    Tell us about your land. Fill out the form or call us. Share the basics — location, acreage, zoning. No pressure, no obligation.
  • 2
    We screen your site. GVA checks your land against car wash developer buy boxes — traffic, frontage, demographics, zoning. You get a clear answer promptly.
  • 3
    We connect you to the developer. If your site qualifies, GVA introduces you directly to a car wash developer ready to issue an LOI and move forward.

GVA charges land sellers no fees. Our compensation is handled privately through our developer relationships — never through the seller.

For Real Estate Agents

Have a Commercial Land Listing? GVA Brings the Developer.

When your commercial land listing could fit car wash development criteria — the right size, location, frontage, and traffic — GVA screens it and works to introduce a qualified car wash developer. You represent your seller. We source the connection.

“We work alongside you — not around you. Your listing relationship is always protected. We bring the developer; you keep your client.”

  • 3
    Your seller meets the developer. GVA introduces a car wash developer directly to your seller. You keep your listing relationship throughout.

GVA does not represent sellers or buyers in a licensed capacity. We are the intermediary that makes the introduction. We work alongside licensed agents — never around them.

About Golden Vision Acquisitions

A Car Wash Land Sourcing Company Built on Integrity

Golden Vision Acquisitions L.L.C. is a Georgia-based commercial land sourcing company specializing exclusively in car wash development sites. We identify, qualify, and connect commercial land to active car wash developers across Georgia and the nation.

We are not a real estate broker. We do not represent buyers or sellers, negotiate contracts, or take listings. GVA's role is to find the land, screen it against developer buy box criteria, and make the introduction. The developer negotiates and closes. GVA handles its own compensation privately through developer relationships — never through the land seller.

We work with land sellers, commercial agents, bird dogs, and wholesalers. If you have a commercial land site that could be a fit for car wash development, GVA wants to hear about it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight Answers, Before You Even Ask

Do you charge landowners any fees?

No. GVA never charges land sellers. Our compensation comes through our developer relationships. You get a free site screening and, if your land fits, a direct introduction to a car wash developer.

Is Golden Vision Acquisitions a real estate brokerage?

No. GVA is not a licensed real estate broker and does not represent buyers or sellers. We are a land sourcing company: we screen sites, match them to developer criteria, and make introductions. Every transaction closes through a licensed title company.

What kind of land are you looking for?

Commercial parcels roughly 0.5–1 acre with 65+ ft of frontage on roads carrying 12,000+ vehicles daily — typically near retail like Walmart, grocery, or fast food. Corner lots are especially attractive.

I'm a real estate agent. Will you work around my listing?

Never. We work alongside agents, not around them. You keep your client and your listing relationship; we bring the developer and sign an NDA before any property details are shared.

Do you only work in Georgia?

Georgia is home base, but we source land for car wash developers nationwide. If you have a site outside Georgia, we still want to hear about it.

How fast do you respond?

Promptly — usually the same business day. Call or text (404) 981-5935, use the contact form below, or book a free 15-minute call.
